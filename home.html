A therapy experience tailored to you

Empathy, patience, and sensitivity set the foundation of care at Valarie Psychological Services.

Valarie Psychological Services is named in honor of Dr. Marshall’s sister, Valarie, who is on the autism spectrum. Inspired by her, Dr. Marshall brings empathy, patience, and sensitivity to his work.

These qualities guide his approach with clients navigating neurodivergence, personality challenges, and emotional difficulties. Dr. Marshall helps clients manage emotions, break free from unhelpful patterns, and improve daily functioning.
