Texas Client Rights & Consumer Information

In accordance with Texas Health and Safety Code §181.105 and Texas House Bill 4224, effective September 1, 2025, Valarie Psychological Services provides the following information regarding your rights as a client, access to your health records, and how to file complaints with the appropriate authorities.

Your Rights as a Client

As a client of this practice, you have the right to:

  • Request access to your health care records maintained by this practice
  • Receive information regarding the confidentiality and privacy of your records
  • Contact the applicable licensing board or disciplinary authority regarding your provider
  • File a consumer complaint with the appropriate state agency
  • Be treated with dignity, respect, and professionalism

Requesting Your Health Care Records

You have the right to request access to or copies of your health care records in accordance with applicable Texas and federal law, including HIPAA.

To request records, please submit a written request that includes:

  • Your full name and date of birth
  • A description of the records requested
  • Your preferred method of delivery (secure electronic copy, mailed copy, etc.)
  • Your signature and date of request
