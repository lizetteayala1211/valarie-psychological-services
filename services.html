Services

We offer a range of services to support our clients with their wellbeing, from assessments, coaching and reports, to group work and seminars.

You can read more about our services and specialties below.

Individual Therapy

One-on-one sessions to help clients navigate life’s challenges, improve emotional regulation, and foster personal growth.

Couples Therapy

Guidance for partners to improve communication, resolve conflicts, and strengthen their relationships.

Family Therapy

Support for families to build understanding, improve dynamics, and navigate life transitions together.

Psychological Assessment

Comprehensive evaluations to clarify diagnoses, guide treatment, and provide insight into cognitive and emotional functioning.

Consultation

Professional consultation services for individuals, families, and organizations seeking psychological expertise.

ADHD/ADD Skills Equip

30-minute skills-based sessions designed to provide clients with tools to manage their ADHD/ADD, learn about their strengths, and improve on their growth edges.

Therapy and Assessment Specialties

Individual therapy, diagnostic assessment, psychometric testing, psychoeducation, and comprehensive clinical reports. Services are delivered with evidence-based care and tailored to individual needs. All performed by Dr. Marshall with a quick turnaround.
